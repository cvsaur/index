BANANA CORP.

Banana Corporation is a Filipino technology company that specializes in consumer electronics, computer software and online services.
Banana is one of the famous information technology company by revenue. As of 2021, Banana Corp. is one of the information technology
companies, alongside Apple, Google (Alphabet), Facebook (Meta), and Microsoft. Banana was founded in 2020 by B1 and B2 to develop
and sell gadget and electronic devices. It was incorporated in 2021, and grew quickly. It went public in summer, to instant financial success.
Over the next few months, Banana shipped new technologies featuring innovative graphical user interfaces and holograms. The company receives
significant criticism regarding the labor practices of its contractors, its environmental practices, and its business ethics, including anti-competitive
behavior and materials sourcing. The company enjoys a high level of brand loyalty, and is ranked as the Philippine's second valuable brand.
